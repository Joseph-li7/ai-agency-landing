FAQ

Common questions

How long does a chatbot take to build?

Most chatbots are live within 3–7 days. The speed depends on how much content you have to train it on. If you have an FAQ or knowledge base, it's fast. If we start from scratch, it takes a bit longer.

Will the chatbot sound like a robot?

No. I train it on your specific tone, products, and brand voice. It sounds like a knowledgeable member of your team, not a generic AI. 70%+ of customers can't tell it's not human.

What if the chatbot gives wrong answers?

I set up a confidence threshold. If the bot isn't sure, it escalates to a human. I also include a review dashboard so you can see what questions it's handling and correct any mistakes in real-time.

Do I need technical skills?

No. Everything I build is designed to be managed without code. You'll get a simple dashboard to update the knowledge base, view analytics, and make changes.

What's the cancellation policy?

Monthly plans can be cancelled anytime with 30 days notice. One-time projects include 30 days of free support after launch. No contracts to sign.
